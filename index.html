You're doing good work, people!

What is it?

“Motivate” is the world's most positive and encouraging IRC bot command. It first appeared in pmxbot on internal IRC at Polimetrix (now YouGov). Other sightings:

  • Urban Airship internal IRC (pmxbot)
  • Bump Technologies internal IRC (bumpbot, pmxbot fork)
  • ZenWraithBot on freenode (zenircbot)
  • SammyTheShark in #digitalocean on freenode (Modubot)
  • Shocky on irc.esper.net (shocky)
  • [o__o] on freenode (botbotme)
  • XOmBot in #xomb on freenode (XOmBot)
  • jenni on freenode (jenni)
  • cfa_cephalopod on #codeforamerica on freenode
  • hubot has it (ported)
  • lita has it (ported)
